k-means Algorithm

Steps

  1. Pick k points. This decides how many clusters we will have.
  2. For each data point, assign to the closest centroid.
  3. Compute new centroids of newly formed clusters after having assigned all data points.
  4. Do steps 2 and 3 again till convergence (centroids and points no longer migrate)

How to pick the right value of k?

Try different values of k and judge average distance to centroid from points as k increases. Plot avergage distance to centroid as k increases.
